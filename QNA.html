Most Asked Questions

Here are the most asked questions about Rocket Gaming Cafe. At Rocket Gaming Cafe, our goal is to create opportunities for future pro gamers and potential streamers of all ages.

Do you need to be a member to buy drinks?

Answer: No, you do not need to be a member to buy drinks.

Does Rocket Gaming Cafe have a gaming space for non-members?

Answer: We have a gaming space for both members and non-members. However, non-members have to pay $20 per five hours they spend.

Can I bring my gaming laptop to the cafe?

Answer: Yes, you can bring your gaming laptop to the cafe.
